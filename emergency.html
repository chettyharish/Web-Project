  • Light Lodge
    For decades Light Lodge has been WildIndia's
    flagship lodge,amed for its warmth, vibrancy
    and legendary hospitality.

    A favourite amongst travellers in search of
    luxury accommodation,Light Lodge is a luxurious
    safari escape set in the heart of the WildIndia
    bushveld overlooking a waterhole and an Indian plain.
  • Water Camp
    Imagine the India of the great safari era, when
    blazing sunsets melted into lantern lit romance
    and service was an effortless whisper.

    This is the India you will experience at WildIndia
    Water Camp, an award winning luxury game lodge in India.
  • Leopard
    The most secretive and elusive of the large carnivores,
    the leopard is also the shrewdest. Pound for pound,
    it is the strongest climber of the large cats and
    capable of killing prey larger than itself.
  • Amani Spa
    Treat yourself to the health and luxury you deserve
    at the Amani Spa at Light Lodge. The perfect place
    to rest and relax, this haven of tranquility offers
    the ultimate African Spa experience.
  • Asian Elephant
    The Majestic Animal of Ancient times with
    footsteps which shake the earth will fill your
    hears with fear.
