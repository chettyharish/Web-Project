WildIndia private game reserve image gallery


Our luxury game lodgeslie within the 65,000 hectare WildIndia Reserve, which is located in the southern section of the Kaziranga National Park, a wildlife paradise; here you can still observe at close quarters the Big 5 and hundreds of other species of Asian animals and birds. During safari drives you will have the opportunity of taking exceptional photographs of the fascinating creatures that live in this bushveld wilderness. Those of you who have visited WildIndia will be reminded of your experiences and for those who have not, the photography will be an enticement to come and experience for yourselves the magic of Sabi Sabi.


ranger galleries

While out on safari your qualified Ranger and Tracker team will interpret the wilderness environment surrounding you. Bringing to life sightings that carry generations of research and folklore, which makes each individual specie unique. A selection of images from some of our Safari Teamare available to view in these galleries - these are moments captured while out on safari by a team passionate about conservation, the environment and showing this pristine world to our guests from all over the world.
