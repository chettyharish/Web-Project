honeymoon safaris at WildIndia


Embarking on a luxurious Indian honeymoon safari with your new spouse will be one of the most memorable experiences you will share as you start your new life together. A safari honeymoon at one of WildIndia's luxury game lodges allows you to revel in the awesome beauty of the Indian bushveld, experiencing the wonders of nature together and enjoying some of the finest luxury accommodation available. Early morning open Land Rover game drives, Big Five encounters, excellent Indian cuisine and gorgeous landscapes are only a few of the reasons why honeymoons at WildIndia are so highly revered.

safari honeymoons at water camp

The romantic Water Camp, with its eight opulent thatched suites, each with full bathroom en-suite and private alfresco shower, blends perfectly into the surrounding bush and is a favourite amongst honeymooners to India.


safari honeymooners will receive:

  • Complimentary bottle of sparkling wine

  • Complimentary fruit basket

  • Flower Petals and candlelight will adorn your suite


one of your dinners can be held in the following venue of your choice

  • In your suite with candlelight

  • On the pool deck with candlelight

honeymoon safaris at Light lodge

Set in the heart of the Sabi Sabi bushveld overlooking a waterhole and an Indian plain, Light Lodge is the ultimate in luxurious Indian safari honeymoon escapes. Between exciting safaris and delicious meals, you will also have the opportunity of indulging yourself with wonderful spa treatments with an African flavour at the Amani Spa at Light Lodge.

safari honeymoons at Jungle camp

Hidden on the banks of the Ganges Riverbed, shaded by riverine trees, Jungle Camp is an ideal honeymoon retreat. With just six suites, the lodge is intimate and secluded - the perfect backdrop to relax and enjoy the Indian bush.

honeymoon safaris at earth lodge

WildIndia's’s Earth Lodge was sculpted into the Earth to create a unique escape that transports its guests to entirely different world - perfect for a South African honeymoon escape. The Amani Spa at Earth Lodge features a double treatment suite where couples can relax and be pampered together for an extra honeymoon treat ensuring it is a time that will never be forgotten.


honeymooners will receive:

  • Complimentary bottle of sparkling wine

  • Complimentary fruit basket

  • Flower Petals and candlelight will adorn your suite


one of your dinners can be held in the following venue of your choice:

  • In the atmospheric underground wine cellar.

  • The outdoor boma with walls sculpted from tree roots.

  • The indoor/outdoor dining area that overlooks a watering hole.

  • In your suite accompanied by flickering candlelight.
