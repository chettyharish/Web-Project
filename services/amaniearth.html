amani spa at earth lodge


While on safari, escape to the Amani Spa at Earth Lodge and enjoy a luxurious facility that reflects the soul of Africa. Revel in a spa experience that is pampering and memorable, relaxing and indulgent. Every detail of the spa at Earth Lodge has been carefully considered.  Natural elements and textures, a sense of peace, space and timeless beauty combine effortlessly with Amani's holistic body, skin and beauty rituals, treatments and massages - all designed to stimulate the body's innate healing processes and naturally inhibit the ageing process. The perfect place to rest, relax and rejuvenate, our unique spa also offers hydrotherapy, a couples treatment suite and a Zen meditation area for quiet and solitude.


At the Amani Spa at Earth Lodge we create magical moments and turn them into special memories. Treat yourself to one of our individually designed treatment packages for any occasion - honeymoon, birthday or anniversary. Share the experience with your partner in the couple's treatment suite. Or simply indulge yourself because you deserve it!

amani spa at earth lodge policy

Treatments are carefully planned to accommodate each and every client. Punctuality will be apprecitated.**. To ensure efficient scheduling of treatments, please check in at the Spa 20 minutes prior to your appointment.

*Treatments for late arrivals will be completed within the scheduled treatment time while the fee will remain unchanged. There will be no cancellation fees should an appointment be cancelled with a minimum of 3 hours notice. Please ensure that any cancellation is made directly with the Spa. A 25% rate surcharge will be levied on treatments booked outside of normal operating hours.
