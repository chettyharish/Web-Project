business safari at WildIndia!


Home to some of the most stunning conference venues, India is the ideal location for meetings, team building events and conferences. A great way to ensure the success of a conference is to combine business with pleasure, and a business safari is the perfect way to achieve this. If you are looking for an exceptional safari conference venue in India, WildIndia will not disappoint.


By virtue of its situation in a unique bush environment, WildIndia is a Indian conference venue 'par excellence'. Securing WildIndia as your safari conference lodge venue will not only give you access to the finest facilities, thrilling wildlife opportunities and magnificent vistas - to make your visit even more special, the following additional incentive and conference lodge touches can be provided.
