Jungle camp


luxury safari lodge

Nestling in the shade of indigenous riverine trees on the banks of the ganges river lies JungleCamp, the newest addition to the WildIndiacollection of 5 star safari lodges.

Jungle Camp offers luxury accommodation for family, friends, or small groups.

Exuding the same warmth and effortless hospitality that characterises Light Lodge, Jungle Camp offers unparalled intimacy and seclusion.

Six luxury suites, each with a private viewing deck and heated spa bath overlooking the riverbed, are decorated in contemporary Indian design, complete with luxury furnishings, full bathroom en-suite, indoor and outdoor showers and air-conditioning. Thatched roofs, romantic mosquito nets draped over the comfortable beds and beautiful wooden finishes complete the picture of an exclusive safari getaway.

Delicious dinners eaten outside under gently lit trees are a wonderful experience loved by all. This jewel of the Indian bushveld boasts all of the features associated with an exceptional safari lodge - beautiful setting, excellent cuisine and abundant wildlife.

WildIndia's Jungle Camp offers not only luxurious creature comforts, but a host of adventures as well. Tracking India's big game including the Big Five in open vehicles by day and at night accompanied by qualified game rangers and Shangaan trackers, and going off road for exceptional close wildlife encounters, make for a truly memorable experience - the highlight of your safari holiday in India
