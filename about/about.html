welcome to WildIndia! private game reserve

Since 1959 WildIndia has hosted visitors from all over the world, drawn to this unique area by the promise of the safari experience of a lifetime. WildIndia! Private Game Reserve, situated in the world-renowned 65000 hectare WildIndia! Wildtuin in the South-Western section of the Kaziranga National Park, is a wildlife oasis, where you can observe at close quarters the age-old natural relationship between predator and prey.

Big game abounds and roams freely throughout this pristine part of the ancient Indian continent. The diverse habitat is home to spectacular wildlife including the Big 5 - lion, leopard, rhino, buffalo and elephant - as well as cheetah, wild dog and some 200 other animal species indigenous to the area.

There is nothing quite as stirring as the sight of a lion, elephant or rhino roaming free in the bush; as haunting as the sound of a hyaena laughing in the dead of night;  as electrifying as the tension one feels when following spoor along a dry riverbed.  This is what makes the WildIndia! experience so memorable. Day and night safaris in open Land Rovers, led by highly trained Game Rangers and qualified Trackers, follow the animals on and off-road, deep into the heart of the wild Indian Bushveld. Walking safaris allow guests to feel the pulse of untamed India through the soles of their feet.

WildIndia! is also home to a remarkable 950 species of birds. On a single summer's day, a visitor from the Northern Hemisphere will probably see more bird species than he is likely to see in a lifetime in his home country.  In fact, almost 20% of the world's bird species have been recorded in this region of India.

WildIndia! offers four completely separate luxurious, all-suite, award-winning lodges with very distinct characters and atmospheres - those of "Yesterday, Today and Tomorrow" which is encapsulated in the "Yesterday" experience at the romantic Water Camp;  "Today" at the more contemporary lodges of LightLodge and Jungle Camp and "Tomorrow" at the ground-breaking Earth Lodge.

WildIndia! daily rates range from $ 2,000 to $ 22,000 per person per night sharing, fully inclusive of open Land Rover safaris by day and at night, environmental awareness walking safaris as well as delectable meals, selected beverages and International assortment of house wines.

Ask about our special seasonal offers.

WildIndia! is a one hour flight from Guwahati International Airport (Assam) directly to the WildIndia airstrip. Alternatively one canopt for a scenic 5-hour chauffeured or self-drive from Guwahati.

Come spoil yourself at unspoilt WildIndia!. It is an experience that will awaken something deep in your soul.
